Highlights

Simple Menus

Use /help in any channel to get a list of commands and features that are accessible. Admins can use /adminhelp

24/7 Online

With a 99.98% uptime in the last 90 days, AntaresBot will always be available to use in your server.

Discord Verified

AntaresBot is a Verified discord bot. We care about your experience and user data privacy and have been verified by discord to be good stewards of your data and their API.

Slash Commands

Using Discord's new "Slash Commands", Antares Bot supports many commands from fun random images, counting and chance games, a counting game, and more; You will never be bored with AntaresBot.

Partner

We partner with Season Bot, a seasonal discord bot, designed to bring holiday fun and games to your server. So far with 11 seasons, 1,784 servers, and 178,3411 users, Season Bot is proven good fun.

Check out Season Bot here: here

Continuous Uptime Monitoring

All services are continuously monitored for performance and uptime. All services automatically restart on failure, and the network admin is notified.
